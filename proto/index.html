NOTES:
  • On the header connector the sequence of the signals is the same of the edge connector EXCEPT the unregulated +12V/-12V power lines.

    The markings on the PCB are accurate.

  • PCB takes also into account the reversal of "R" and "T" signals in the original schematic. The markings on the PCB are accurate.
